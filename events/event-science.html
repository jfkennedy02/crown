Young Scientists Fair

Showcasing innovation and curiosity from our future leaders. A celebration of scientific discovery and creative problem-solving.

About the Fair

The Young Scientists Fair is Crown Heights Academy's premier STEM event, where students from all grade levels get the opportunity to demonstrate their scientific curiosity and problem-solving skills. This year, we're expecting over 50 exciting projects from our budding scientists!

Project Categories

  • Biology & Life Sciences - From plant growth experiments to animal behavior studies
  • Chemistry - Safe and exciting chemical reactions and materials science
  • Physics & Engineering - Bridges, catapults, and energy experiments
  • Environmental Science - Sustainability projects and eco-friendly innovations
  • Robotics & Technology - Coding projects and mechanical inventions

Special Guests

This year, we're honored to have special guest judges from the University of Port Harcourt's Science Faculty. They will evaluate projects and award prizes in each category.
