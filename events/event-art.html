Creative Arts Exhibition

Celebrating color, imagination, and artistic expression. Join us for a vibrant showcase of student creativity across multiple art forms.

A Celebration of Creativity

The Creative Arts Exhibition is Crown Heights Academy's annual celebration of the arts. This event provides our students with a platform to showcase their artistic talents and creative expressions in various mediums.

Featured Art Forms

Painting

Oil, Acrylic, Watercolor

Drawing

Sketches & Portraits

Sculpture

Clay & Mixed Media

Photography

Digital & Film

This Year's Theme

"Our Beautiful Nigeria" - Students have been encouraged to create artworks that celebrate Nigerian culture, landscapes, traditions, and the beauty of our diverse heritage. From Lagos skylines to village scenes, expect a colorful journey through our great nation.

Live Performances

The exhibition will also feature live performances including:

  • Traditional dance performances by the Cultural Club
  • Poetry readings by the Literary Society
  • Live painting demonstrations by senior art students
  • Musical performances by the school choir

Schedule

Date: May 20, 2026
Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Venue: Crown Heights Academy Art Gallery & Courtyard

All parents, guardians, and art enthusiasts are warmly invited. Selected artworks will be available for purchase, with proceeds going to the school's Art Scholarship Fund.

Art Competition

Students can still submit entries until May 10th. Visit the Art Department for submission guidelines.
