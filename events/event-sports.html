March 15, 2026

Annual Inter-House Sports

A day of energy, teamwork, and healthy competition as students from all four houses battle for glory on the field.

Event Highlights

The Annual Inter-House Sports is one of the most anticipated events at Crown Heights Academy. This year's edition promises to be bigger and better, featuring a wide range of athletic competitions designed to showcase the talents and sportsmanship of our students.

Competitions

  • 100m, 200m, and 400m races
  • Relay races (4x100m)
  • Long jump and high jump
  • Shot put and javelin throw
  • Sack race and egg-and-spoon race (for younger students)

The Four Houses

Our students are divided into four competitive houses, each with its own colors and spirit:

Red House
Blue House
Yellow House
Green House

Schedule

Date: March 15, 2026
Time: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Venue: Crown Heights Academy Sports Field

Parents and guardians are warmly invited to attend and cheer on our young athletes. Refreshments will be available, and there will be special performances by the school band during breaks.

Important Notice

All participating students should arrive by 7:30 AM for warm-up and registration. PE kits are mandatory.
