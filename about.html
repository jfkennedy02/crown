Who We Are

Our Story & Mission

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment where every child is encouraged to explore, create, and excel, fostering a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

To be the leading nursery and primary institution in Rivers State, known for raising confident, compassionate, and competent future leaders.

Our Journey

2010

Inception

Crown Heights Academy was founded with just 15 pupils and a vision to transform education in Woji.

2015

Expansion

We expanded our facilities to include a modern science lab, library, and art studio, growing to 200+ pupils.

2020

Digital Leap

Introduced ICT-enhanced learning and coding classes for primary admission, setting a new standard.

2026

Today

Recognized as a center of excellence with award-winning students and a vibrant community.
